About Roseau

Nestled in a serene landscape, Roseau Hotel & Spa combines timeless elegance with modern comforts. Enjoy beautifully appointed rooms, exceptional service, and a peaceful environment that feels like home away from home. Founded in 2010, Roseau has become a landmark of hospitality. We take pride in offering world-class spa treatments, breathtaking views, and personalized service to every guest.
